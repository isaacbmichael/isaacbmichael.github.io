Work with me

Outcome-driven, fixed-scope engagements. I turn complex survey and operational data into audit-ready, reproducible workflows and decision briefs that leaders can trust.

Typical focus areas: nonresponse bias and weighting (e.g., PRAMS), analytics automation (SAS/R/Python + QA gates), data modernization roadmaps, and executive dashboards with clear thresholds.

I prefer lightweight sprints over endless meetings. You get clear deliverables, documented methods, and a clean handoff—no bureaucracy, no timesheets.

Core offers

Nonresponse Bias Audit (2-week sprint)

Outcome: A decision-ready brief: whether bias is material, where it comes from, and what to do next.

  • Diagnostics & risk screen; design vs. nonresponse vs. coverage
  • Mitigation plan: reweight, recontact, or caveat
  • Executive summary + technical appendix (reproducible)

Survey Weighting & QA Setup (3-week sprint)

Outcome: Reproducible weights with a documented QA path (design → nonresponse → poststratification).

  • Parameterised SAS/R weighting code + parity checks
  • Automated QA gates & sampling-verification templates
  • One-click regeneration of standard outputs

Data Modernization Roadmap (4-week engagement)

Outcome: A pragmatic path from “forms + manual tables” to versioned pipelines.

  • Current-state map & target architecture
  • Phased plan with risks, staffing, and cost bands
  • Governance: SLAs, ownership, and audit trail

Analytics Quality Gates Install (2-week sprint)

Outcome: Fewer surprises in production tables/dashboards.

  • Configurable checks: schema, validity, coverage, drift
  • Failure playbooks + alerts and scorecards
  • Documentation so teams can extend safely

Decision Dashboard + Brief (2-week sprint)

Outcome: An executive-ready dashboard with a two-page interpretation memo tied to thresholds & actions.

  • Metric dictionary + decisions/alerts table
  • Accessible visuals and exportable artifacts
  • Handoff with maintainers’ checklist

Advisory Retainer (lightweight)

Outcome: Ongoing, async support for your analytics lead—without the overhead.

  • Monthly office hours & rapid reviews
  • Scoping, vendor sanity checks, hiring support
  • Quarterly health check on pipelines & QA

How it works

1) Fit check (15 min)

Goals, constraints, data availability. If we’re not a fit, I’ll point you to a better option.

2) Scope & quote (48h)

One-page scope with deliverables, timeline, and a fixed price. No timesheets.

3) Sprint & handoff

Weekly async update; final readout with documentation, artifacts, and next steps.

Representative outcomes

National weighting at scale

Co-led weighting across 50 PRAMS jurisdictions (~81% of U.S. live births) with modular SAS/SQL, automated QA, and standard outputs.

Bias work that shaped policy

Evidence and guidance on nonresponse bias supported OMB’s move toward quality-focused evaluation over rigid response-rate minimums.

Program enablement & reporting

Built multi-program weighting + automated reporting; created sampling-verification templates and checks that shortened cycles and raised confidence.
